    预积分推导

    预积分的作用¶

    因为优化的过程中，有可能改变先前的状态，为了不每次都重新进行积分，希望能将两个状态\(x_t\) 和 \(x_{t+1}\)之间的变化量（差值）用t时刻之后的IMU的观测描述出来：

    \[ x_{t+\Delta t}=x_{t}+\Delta x_t(y_t) \]

    动力学积分模型¶

    \[ \begin{align} \dot{R}&=R \cdot [\omega]_{\times} &R_{k+1}&=R_kExp(w_k \Delta t)\\ \dot{v}&=a &\stackrel{积分}{\Longrightarrow} v_{k+1}&=v_k+a_k\Delta t\\ \dot{p}&=v& p_{k+1}&=p_k+v_k \Delta t+\frac{1}{2}a_k\Delta t^2 \end{align} \]

    用IMU测量值 \(\tilde{(\cdot)}\) 表示真实值:

    \[ \begin{align} \tilde{\omega}&=\omega+b_g+\eta_g &\Rightarrow \omega&=\tilde{\omega}-b_g-\eta_g\\ \tilde{a}&=R^T(a-g)+b_a+\eta_a &\Rightarrow a&= R(\tilde{a}-b_a-\eta_a)+g \end{align} \]

    用测量值代入积分模型（单帧）：

    \[ \begin{align} R_{k+1}&=R_kExp((\tilde{\omega}_{k}-b_{g,k}-\eta_{dg,k})\Delta t)\\ v_{k+1}&=v_k+R_k(\tilde{a}-b_a-\eta_{da,k})\Delta t+g\Delta t\\ p_{k+1}&=p_k+v_k \Delta t+\frac{1}{2}a_k\Delta t^2 \\ Cov (\eta_{gd} )&= Cov( \eta_{g}/\Delta t )\\ Cov (\eta_{ad} )&= Cov( \eta_{a}/\Delta t ) \end{align} \]

    离散噪声项

    Sigma-point Kalman filtering for integrated GPS and inertial navigation

    \[ \begin{align} \tilde{\omega}(t)&=\omega(t)+b_g(t)+\eta_g(t) \\ \dot{b_g}(t)&=\eta_u(t)\\ \frac{1}{\Delta t} \int_{t_0}^{t_0+\Delta t}\tilde{\omega}(t)dt &= \frac{1}{\Delta t} \int_{t_0}^{t_0+\Delta t}[\omega(t)+b_g(t)+\eta_g(t)]dt \end{align} \]

    假设真值和测量值在采样后保持不变，积分时可作为常数处理：

    \[ \frac{1}{\Delta t} \int_{t_0}^{t_0+\Delta t}[\omega(t)+b_g(t)+\eta_g(t)]dt \]

    预积分模型¶

    通常将两个关键帧(k=i 到 k=j)内所有测量合并，称为预积分：

    \[ \begin{align} \Delta t_{ij} &\triangleq \sum_{k=i}^{j-1} \Delta t\\ R_{j}&=R_i \prod_{k=i}^{j-1} Exp((\tilde{\omega}_{k}-b_{g,k}-\eta_{gd,k})\Delta t)\\ v_{j}&=v_i+\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}R_k(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t+g \Delta t_{ij}\\ p_{j}&=p_i+\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}v_k \Delta t+\frac{1}{2}g \Delta t_{ij}^2+\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}R_k(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t^2 \end{align} \]

    其中比较麻烦的的便是这个\(R_i\)和\(v_i\),代表的是k时刻的姿态，会影响到后续的计算，因此引出了预积分的定义，希望能消除\(R_i\)和\(v_i\)的影响：

    \[ \begin{align} \Delta R_{ij} &\triangleq R_i^\top R_j\\ &=\prod_{k=i}^{j-1} Exp((\tilde{\omega}_{k}-b_{g,k}-\eta_{gd,k})\Delta t)\\ \Delta v_{ij} &\triangleq R_i^\top (v_j-v_i-g\Delta t_{ij})\\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta R_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t \\ \Delta p_{ij} &\triangleq R_i^\top (p_j-p_i-v_i\Delta t_{ij}-\frac{1}{2}g \Delta t_{ij}^2)\\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}[\Delta v_{ik} \Delta t +\frac{1}{2}\Delta R_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t^2] \end{align} \]

    四元数性质

    \[ \begin{align} Exp((\phi+\Delta \phi)^{\wedge})&=Exp(\phi^{\wedge})Exp(J_r\Delta \phi^{\wedge})\\ &=Exp(J_l\Delta \phi^{\wedge})Exp(\phi^{\wedge})\\ J_l(\theta u)&=\frac{sin\theta}{\theta}I+(1-\frac{sin\theta}{\theta})uu^\top+\frac{1-cos\theta}{\theta}a^{\wedge}\\ 伴随性质：R^\top Exp(\phi) R &= Exp(R^\top \phi)\\ \Rightarrow Exp(\phi) R &= R Exp(R^\top \phi) \end{align} \]

    上面推导得出的差值量中仍然含有i时刻的噪声项影响，我们可以利用李代数的性质将它分离开： - 旋转部分：

    \[ \begin{align} \Delta R_{ij} &=\prod_{k=i}^{j-1} \underline{Exp((\tilde{\omega}_{k}-b_{g,k})\Delta t-\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t)}\\ &=\prod_{k=i}^{j-1} \underbrace{Exp((\tilde{\omega}_{k}-b_{g,k})\Delta t)}_{\triangleq \Delta \tilde{R}_{ij}}\quad\underline{Exp(-J_{r,k}\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t)}\\ &= \dots \Delta \tilde{R}_{j-2,j-1} \underbrace{Exp(-J_{r,j-1}\eta_{gd,j-1}\Delta t) \Delta \tilde{R}_{j-1,j}}_{\Delta \tilde{R}_{j-2,j} Exp(-\tilde{R}_{j-1,j}^\top J_{r,j-1}\eta_{gd,j-1}\Delta t) } Exp(-J_{r,j}\eta_{gd,j}\Delta t) \\ &=\dots \Delta \tilde{R}_{j-2,j} Exp(-\tilde{R}_{j-1,j}^\top J_{r,j-1}\eta_{gd,j-1}\Delta t) Exp(-J_{r,j}\eta_{gd,j}\Delta t)\\ &=\Delta \tilde{R}_{ij} \prod_{k=i}^{j-1} Exp(-\tilde{R}_{k+1,j}^\top J_{r,k}\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t)\\ &\triangleq \Delta \tilde{R}_{ij} Exp(-\delta \phi_{ij}) \end{align} \]
    • 速度部分：
    \[ \begin{align} \Delta v_{ij} &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta R_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t \\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}Exp(-\delta \phi_{ik})(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t\\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}(I-\delta \phi_{ik}^{\wedge})(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t\\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}-\eta_{ad,k})\Delta t -\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}[\delta \phi_{ik}^{\wedge}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k})-\cancel{\delta \phi_{ik}^{\wedge}\eta_{ad,k}}]\Delta t\\ &=\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\underbrace{\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k})\Delta t}_{\Delta \tilde{v}_{ij}}-\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}\eta_{ad,k}\Delta t +\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}[\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}]^{\wedge}\delta \phi_{ik}\Delta t\\ &=\Delta \tilde{v}_{ij}+\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}[\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k}]^{\wedge}\delta \phi_{ik}\Delta t -\Delta \tilde{R}_{ik}\eta_{ad,k}\Delta t\\ &=\Delta \tilde{v}_{ij}-\delta v_{ij} \end{align} \]
    • 位移部分同理：
    \[ \begin{align} \Delta \tilde{p}_{ij} &= \sum_{k=i}^{j-1}[(\Delta \tilde{v}_{ij}\Delta t)+\frac{1}{2}\tilde{R}_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k})\Delta t^2]\\ \Delta p_{ij} &= \Delta \tilde{p}_{ij}+\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}[-\delta v_{ik}\Delta t + \frac{1}{2}\tilde{R}_{ik}(\tilde{a}_k-b_{a,k})^{\wedge}\delta \phi_{ik}\Delta t^2-\frac{1}{2}\tilde{R}_{ik}\eta_{ad,k}\Delta t^2]\\ &=\Delta \tilde{p}_{ij} - \delta p_{ij} \end{align} \]

    测量值计算¶

    重新将定义式代入可以得到测量值的计算公式：

    \[ \begin{align} \Delta \tilde{R}_{ij} Exp(-\delta \phi_{ij}) &= \Delta R_{ij} \triangleq R_i^\top R_j \\ \Rightarrow \Delta \tilde{R}_{ij} &= \Delta R_i^\top R_j Exp(\delta \phi_{ij})\\ \Delta \tilde{v}_{ij}-\delta v_{ij} &=\Delta v_{ij} \triangleq R_i^\top (v_j-v_i-g\Delta t_{ij}) \\ \Rightarrow \Delta \tilde{v}_{ij} &=\Delta v_{ij} \triangleq R_i^\top (v_j-v_i-g\Delta t_{ij})+\delta v_{ij}\\ \Delta \tilde{p}_{ij} - \delta p_{ij} &=\Delta p_{ij} \triangleq R_i^\top (p_j-p_i-v_i\Delta t_{ij}-g \Delta t_{ij}) \\ \Rightarrow \Delta \tilde{p}_{ij}&=\Delta p_{ij} \triangleq R_i^\top (p_j-p_i-v_i\Delta t_{ij}-g \Delta t_{ij}) + \delta p_{ij}\\ \end{align} \]

    噪声项计算¶

    旋转噪声：

    \[ \begin{align} Exp(-\delta \phi_{ij}) &= \prod_{k=i}^{j-1} Exp(-\tilde{R}_{k+1,j}^\top J_{r,k}\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t)\\ \delta \phi_{ij} &= -Log(\prod_{k=i}^{j-1}Exp(-\tilde{R}_{k+1,j}^\top J_{r,k}\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t))\\ &\simeq\sum_{k=i}^{j-1}\tilde{R}_{k+1,j}^\top J_{r,k}\eta_{gd,k}\Delta t\\ & \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\Sigma) \end{align} \]

    Warning

    上面\(\delta \phi\)中第二步, 一般来说在SO3上并不满足\(Exp(\sum a_i)=\prod Exp(a_i)\)，严格来说

    \[ Exp(a)Exp(b)=Exp(J_L^{-1}a+b)=Exp(a+J_R^{-1}b) \]

    由于a,b都很小，因此，J_Ｒ 近似为单位阵。

    因此三个噪声项\(\delta \phi_{ij} \(,\)\delta v_{ij}\)和\(\delta p_{ij}\)都满足0均值高斯分布。

    实际计算中，更为高效的方法是使用迭代的方式，而不是每次计算所有的累加。

    \[ \begin{align} \delta \Phi_{i,j} &= \Delta \tilde{R}^\top_{j-1,j}\delta \Phi_{i,j-1}+J_r^{j-1}\eta^{gd}_{j-1}\Delta t\\ \delta v_{ij}&=\delta v_{i,j-1}- \Delta \tilde{R}^\top_{i,j-1}(\tilde a_{j-1}-b_i^a)^{\wedge}\delta \phi_{i,j-1} \Delta t + \Delta \tilde{R}_{i,j-1}\eta^{ad}_{j-1}\Delta t\\ \delta p_{i,j}&=\delta p_{i,j-1}+\delta v_{i,j-1}\Delta t- \frac{1}{2} \Delta \tilde{R}^\top_{i,j-1}(\tilde a_{j-1}-b_i^a)^{\wedge}\delta \phi_{i,j-1} \Delta t^2 + \frac{1}{2} \Delta \tilde{R}_{i,j-1}\eta^{ad}_{j-1}\Delta t^2 \\ \Rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} \delta \Phi_{i,k+1}\\ \delta v_{i,k+1}\\ \delta p_{i,k+1} \end{bmatrix}&= \begin{bmatrix} \Delta \tilde{R}_{k,k+1} & 0_{3 \times 3} & 0_{3 \times 3}\\ - \Delta \tilde{R}^\top_{i,k}(\tilde a_{j-1}-b_i^a)^{\wedge}\Delta t & I_{3 \times 3} & 0_{3 \times 3}\\ - \frac{1}{2}\Delta \tilde{R}^\top_{i,k}(\tilde a_{j-1}-b_i^a)^{\wedge}\Delta t^2 & I_{3 \times 3}\Delta t & 0_{3 \times 3}\\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \delta \Phi_{i,k}\\ \delta v_{i,k}\\ \delta p_{i,k} \end{bmatrix}+ \begin{bmatrix} J_r^{k}\Delta t& 0_{3 \times 3}\\ 0_{3 \times 3} & \Delta \tilde{R}_{i,k}\Delta t\\ 0_{3 \times 3} & \frac{1}{2}\Delta \tilde{R}_{i,k}\Delta t^2 \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}\eta^{gd}_{k}\\\eta^{ad}_{k}\end{bmatrix}\\ \Rightarrow \eta_{i,k+1}&=A\eta_{i,k}+B\eta^{d}_{k} \end{align} \]

    由此，协方差也可以迭代计算得到：

    \[ \Sigma_{i,k+1}=A\Sigma_{i,k}A^\top+B\Sigma_{\eta}B^\top \]
